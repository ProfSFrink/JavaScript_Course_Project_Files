Live A Live
29 days ago

A 2D-HD remake of the unknow Super Famicom RPG which never left Japan and the only way to play it in the west was through a fan-translation. A fantastic remake with a psuedo real-time strategy battle system. Play as one of eight different characters through different time periods, starting in the pre-historic era, then moving onto edo Japan, the wild west and even outer space in the future.

Developed by Square-Enix - Published by Nintendo.
Xenoblade Chronicles 3
22 days ago

The third game in the Xenoblade Chronicles series follows a group of child solider who are creating purely to fight in a war they have no choice in. Features the same MMO-style combat of the prior games along with the open-world exploration this time in the new setting of aionis.

Developed by Monolith Software - Published by Nintendo.
The Wraithbone Phoenix
1 days ago

Baggit is the fast-talking ratling with an eye for the big score. Clodde is the ogryn with a heart of gold and a head full of dreams. These abhuman deserters-turned-criminals operating in the city of Varangantua have crossed the wrong man for the last time – and now they’re on the run… At least, that is, until Baggit learns of a score that could pay their debts and save their skins – the Wraithbone Phoenix, a fabled treasure rumoured to be hidden aboard a voidship recently scheduled for decommission. But Baggit and Clodde aren’t the only ones with eyes on the prize. When word gets out, every thief in the Dredge is going to be gunning for the Phoenix. Does Baggit have the cunning to find the treasure and get out alive, or will someone finally cash in the bounty on his head?

Written by Alex Worley - Published by The Black Library.
